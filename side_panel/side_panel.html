Reader's Aide

How to Use Reader's Aide

  1. Type a word in the search bar
  2. Click "GO!" or press enter to get the definition
  3. Click the volume icon to hear the word
  4. Click the microphone icon to practice pronouncing the word

Press the "Info" button again to close this and get started!
